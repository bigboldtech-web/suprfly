Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address, name, and password (hashed). If you sign up via Google OAuth, we receive your name and email from Google.

Connected Platform Data: When you connect LinkedIn or X via our Chrome Extension, we capture session cookies necessary to post comments on your behalf. These cookies are encrypted using AES-256-GCM and stored securely. We do not access your passwords, direct messages, or private content.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use Suprfly, including keywords configured, creators targeted, comments generated, and analytics data. This helps us improve the service.

Payment Information: Payment processing is handled by Stripe and Razorpay. We do not store your credit card numbers. We receive transaction confirmations and subscription status from these providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Suprfly service
  • Generate AI-powered comments on your behalf using your configured voice profile
  • Post comments to LinkedIn and X using your connected sessions
  • Send transactional emails (session alerts, daily summaries, billing notifications)
  • Improve our AI comment quality and content safety systems
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit. Session cookies are encrypted using AES-256-GCM with keys stored separately from the encrypted data. We use industry-standard security practices including firewalls, fail2ban, and regular security updates.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • AI Providers: Post content (not your personal data) is sent to AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI) to generate comments. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.
  • Payment Processors: Stripe and Razorpay process your payments.
  • Infrastructure Providers: Our hosting, database, and email service providers process data as necessary to operate the service.

5. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. The Chrome Extension captures LinkedIn/X cookies solely for the purpose of posting comments on your behalf.

6. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format (CSV)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential emails
  • Restrict Processing: Pause AI commenting at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@suprfly.io.

7. Children's Privacy

Suprfly is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries:

  • Email: privacy@suprfly.io
  • Website: suprfly.io/contact
