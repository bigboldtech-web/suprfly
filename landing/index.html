Will my account get banned?+

No. Suprfly uses human-pattern simulation with randomized delays, respects platform rate limits, and operates within safe engagement boundaries. Zero bans across 10,000+ users.

How does the AI sound human?+

We use advanced language models that analyze each post's context, reference specific content, and match your voice profile. No generic "great post!" comments — ever.

Can I review comments first?+

Yes! Enable "Manual Approval Mode" and every comment goes to your review queue before posting. Or run full autopilot with AI safety guardrails.

What languages are supported?+

25+ languages. Suprfly auto-detects the post language and responds natively — English, Hindi, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Portuguese, and more.

Does it comment on sensitive posts?+

Never. Our two-layer content safety system (keyword filter + AI classification) automatically skips political, discriminatory, tragic, or controversial content.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-ins. Cancel from your dashboard in seconds. Your data is fully exportable too.
