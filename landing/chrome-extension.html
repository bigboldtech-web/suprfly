Privacy & Security
YOUR SECURITY COMES FIRST
We only access what we need and nothing more.

What We Access

  • Session cookies for LinkedIn and X (to authenticate API requests)
  • Browser tab URL (to detect when you're on LinkedIn or X)
  • Extension popup interaction (to trigger connect/disconnect)

What We DON'T Access

  • Your passwords or login credentials
  • Your direct messages or private conversations
  • Your personal data, contacts, or address book
  • Any browsing history or activity outside LinkedIn and X
  • Any data from other tabs or websites
Questions
EXTENSION FAQ
Is the extension safe to use? +

Yes. The extension only accesses session cookies for LinkedIn and X — never your passwords, DMs, or personal data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. The extension is reviewed and approved by the Chrome Web Store.

Does it work in incognito mode? +

By default, Chrome extensions are disabled in incognito mode. You can enable it manually in your extension settings, but we recommend using the extension in normal browsing mode for the best experience.

How do I reconnect if my session expires? +

Simply log back into LinkedIn or X in your browser, then open the Suprfly extension popup and click Reconnect. The extension will sync your new session automatically.

Does it work on Firefox or other browsers? +

Currently the extension is available for Google Chrome and Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Arc). A Firefox version is on our roadmap. Join our waitlist to be notified when it launches.
