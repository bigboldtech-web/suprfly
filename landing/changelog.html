WHAT'S NEW

All the latest features, improvements, and fixes shipped to Suprfly.

March 15, 2026
v1.4.0 — Feature

Multi-Language Comment Generation

Suprfly now auto-detects the language of the original post and generates comments in the same language. Supports 25+ languages including English, Hindi, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Portuguese, Arabic, and more. No configuration needed — it just works.

March 1, 2026
v1.3.0 — Feature

Prompt Builder & Custom AI Instructions

You can now give the AI custom instructions, add example comments you like, and specify topics to avoid. The new Prompt Builder page in the dashboard gives you full control over how the AI writes on your behalf.

February 20, 2026
v1.2.2 — Improvement

Faster Post Discovery

Reduced post discovery interval from 15 minutes to 10 minutes. Creator posts are now detected within 5 minutes of publication. This means your comments appear faster, increasing the likelihood of being seen by the post's audience.

February 10, 2026
v1.2.0 — Feature

Analytics Dashboard & CSV Export

Track your growth with the new analytics dashboard. See followers, connections, and comments over time with interactive charts. Filter by date range (10/30/90/180 days) and export all activity data as CSV for your own analysis.

January 28, 2026
v1.1.1 — Fix

Session Refresh Reliability

Fixed an issue where session cookies would occasionally fail to refresh, causing temporary disconnections. The Chrome Extension now performs pre-emptive refresh checks and provides clearer reconnection prompts when sessions expire.

January 15, 2026
v1.1.0 — Feature

X (Twitter) Support

Suprfly now supports X (formerly Twitter) in addition to LinkedIn. Connect your X account via the Chrome Extension and start auto-replying to tweets matching your keywords and target creators. Thread-aware commenting ensures contextual replies.

January 1, 2026
v1.0.0 — Feature

Suprfly Launch

The initial release of Suprfly with LinkedIn support, AI comment generation, keyword and creator targeting, tone settings, content safety filter, manual approval mode, and the Chrome Extension for session management.
