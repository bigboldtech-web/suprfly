FAQ

Pricing FAQ

Common questions about plans, billing, and limits.

Can I switch plans? +

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated — you only pay the difference.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. Annual plans can also be paid by invoice for teams and agencies.

Is there a contract? +

No contracts, no commitments. All plans are month-to-month (or annual if you choose the discounted option). Cancel anytime with one click from your dashboard.

What happens when I hit my daily comment limit? +

Suprfly pauses commenting for the day and sends you a notification. There are no overage charges. Your limit resets at midnight UTC, or you can upgrade your plan anytime to increase it.
